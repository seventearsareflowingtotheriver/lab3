Nov 25, 2025 Major Victory
Liberation of Salt Mine #4

Liberation of Salt Mine #4

Our brave Driller corps managed to collapse a key smuggling tunnel used by Russian elves, burying their supply lines under tons of glorious rock.

The operation, codenamed "Salty Surprise", was executed with surgical precision using C4 charges. The subsequent cavern celebration reportedly caused mild seismic activity in the region. Local wildlife has been secured, and beer production has resumed at full capacity.

Casualties: None (except for a few hangovers).
Loot secured: 5000 units of Nitra.

Nov 20, 2025 Tech Update
New Bosco Units

New Anti-Elf Bosco Units Deployed

R&D has retrofitted our Bosco drones with enhanced sensors to detect pointy-ear magic signatures in the Donetsk sector.

Early field tests show a 400% increase in "accidental" discharges against hostile targets disguised as trees. The new firmware update v4.2 also allows Bosco to salute "Rock and Stone" upon a successful kill.

Miners are advised not to stand directly in front of Bosco during calibration protocols.

Nov 15, 2025 Hero Spotlight
Interview with Gimli

Interview: Karl's Spiritual Successor?

"These elves fight like leaf-lovers," says veteran Gunner 'Bore-Master' Gimli. "A little bit of minigun fire and they scatter."

"The real challenge is finding enough ammo for the whole horde. Back on Hoxxes, we had bugs. Here, we have orcs and elves. Different skin, same satisfying crunch when the Drop Pod lands on them."

Gimli was later seen arm-wrestling a Driller for the last round of Oily Oaf Brew. He won.
